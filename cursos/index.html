Welcome!

I am a Bayesian Statistician and Data Scientist. In August 2015 I joined the Departent of Actuarial Science and Insurance at ITAM as a Full Time Professor in Actuarial Statistics. I teach courses in aggregate loss modeling, ruin and credibility theory and data science. For many years I have worked solving real life problems in the Social Sciences, with applications covering topics on macroeconomic modelling, forecasting, causality, classification methods and inference on finite populations. Some of my professional experience is summarized in this [link].

Currently, I am interested in studying different aspects of Bayesian Statistics, with particular emphasis on Nonparametric Methods, Time-series Modelling, Actuarial Statistics, Quantitative Risk Modelling, Macro-Econometrics, Computational Statistics, and in general any aspect of Bayesian statistics aimming at solving real life problems.

I am also interested in studying contemporaneous topics on Data Science, in particular aspects related to Visualization of Complex Data and Scalability of Computational Procedures.
