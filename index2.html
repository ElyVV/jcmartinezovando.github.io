Welcome!

I am a Bayesian Statistician and Data ScientistI am a Full-Time Professor at the Departent of Actuarial Science and Insurance at ITAM.

Interests

My interest lie on the wide area of Bayesian Statistics, with particular enphasis on Nonparametric Methods, Time-series Modelling, Actuarial Statistics, Quantitative Risk Modelling, Macro-Econometrics, Computational Statistics, and in general any aspect of Bayesian statistics aimming at solving real life problems.

Currently, I am also interested in Data Science, in particular aspects related to Visualization of Complex Data and Scalability of Computational Procedures.
