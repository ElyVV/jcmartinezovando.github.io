Announcements:

Fields of interest

I am interestd in Bayesian statistics, Econometrics and quantitative methods in the social sciences. More specifically, time series analysis, Bayesian non- and semi-parametric methods, model selection, inflation forecasting and dynamic equilibrium models.

My work is being supervised by Prof Stephen G Walker. Currently, we are working on the construction and characterization of stationary processes with flexible (non- or semi-parametric) components. I gratefully acknowledge CONACyT (Mexican Government) for funding my research.

Work in progress

  2. A note on the construction and inference of stationary sequences (with S. G. Walker).

  4. Bayesian estimation of flexible technical progress functions and the elasticity of substitution (with M. León-Ledesma, P. McAdam, S. G. Walker and A. Willman).
